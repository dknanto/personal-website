Background

David has over 25 years experience working for Japanese companies including over 15 years working in the Corporate Strategy Division of one of Japan's largest conglomerates. Over his career, he has worked in Tokyo, New York, and Boston.

In his current role as the Head of Corporate Branding - US, David is responsible for global brand strategies and the CEO's global communication strategy. Prior to this role, he developed corporate-wide transformation strategies for top executives in Tokyo.

David has worked with operating presidents in the US and EMEA on growth strategies, business model analysis, and organizational design.
