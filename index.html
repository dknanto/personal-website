About

An artist’s sensibility with an operator’s discipline.

David’s work moves between creative leadership and strategic consulting. On the creative side, he writes, directs, and produces theater with a strong grounding in language, performance, and audience connection.

On the business side, he brings experience in brand strategy, speechwriting, and Japan-focused advisory work, helping ideas travel clearly across industries, markets, and cultures.
